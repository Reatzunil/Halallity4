Establishment

Peñalver Food and Refreshment Hub

The establishment offers traditional Filipino delicacies such as 'kakanin' alongside a compact carinderia setup.

  • LOCATION: Poblacion 8, Midsayap, North Cotabato
  • CONTACT NO.: 0908 547 7864
  • FACEBOOK: https://www.facebook.com/PFRH008

'Peñalver' began by occasionally making and selling 'kakanin', but as demand increased, it grew into a successful business. Starting small, their love for making 'kakanin' turned into a thriving venture, sharing Filipino delicacies with more people.
